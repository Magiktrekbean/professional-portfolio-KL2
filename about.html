About Me

Crafting User-Centered Web Experiences with Passion and Precision

Hello! I’m Kyla, a passionate front-end developer based in Lexington, Kentucky, with a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I’ve honed my skills through the SheCodes program, progressing from a complete beginner to an intermediate developer, and am currently expanding my expertise with SheCodes Responsive. My journey has equipped me with the tools to build interactive, user-centered websites that are not only functional but also visually engaging.

Through courses like SheCodes Basics, SheCodes Basics Add-On, SheCodes Plus, and now SheCodes Responsive, I’ve gained hands-on experience with a variety of projects. This experience has sharpened my technical abilities and deepened my understanding of responsive web design, GitHub, APIs, and hosting.

I pride myself on my problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to create polished, high-quality web applications. My goal is to continue growing as a developer while delivering engaging, user-friendly experiences. Whether you’re a tech company or a freelancer looking for a dedicated developer, I’m ready to bring your ideas to life and help you build impactful, well-crafted web solutions.

As a developer, I believe that the best web applications are built through collaboration. I enjoy working closely with clients to understand their needs, goals, and vision for a project. Whether it’s crafting a custom website for a business or developing an engaging web app, my approach is always rooted in clear communication and problem-solving. I aim to create solutions that not only meet your expectations but exceed them, ensuring that every project is user-centered and aligned with your brand’s objectives. I’m always excited about bringing fresh ideas to the table and using my skills to help clients achieve their vision.

If you’re looking for a front-end developer who is passionate, reliable, and committed to delivering top-notch work, let’s connect! I’m always eager to take on new challenges and work with clients who are as excited about their projects as I am. Reach out today to discuss how I can help bring your ideas to life with clean, functional, and visually appealing web solutions. I look forward to working with you!
